## Visual Hierarchy

**Primary Focal Point:** Age verification modal overlay on first launch

The age verification modal is the visual anchor for Phase 1. It blocks all other interactions until the user confirms being 18+ or exits. Design emphasizes:
- Centered modal with subtle shadow
- Prominent "确认已满18岁" CTA button in accent green
- Clear exit option for underage users
